How to use LingoCards
  • Swipe, tap the arrows, or use keyboard arrow keys to move between cards
  • Tap the category tabs at the top to explore topics like animals, food, or numbers
  • Alphabet cards show both upper and lowercase letters
  • Days and months show a calendar tile — the small segments at the bottom mark its position in the sequence
  • Tap the speaker button to hear the word pronounced
  • Tap ⚙️ five times quickly to open parent settings — filter cards by difficulty level or change language
